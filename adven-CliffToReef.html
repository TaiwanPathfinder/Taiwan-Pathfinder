Cliff to Reef — One-Day Rappel & Climb in Longdong

6–7 hours of adventure (including rock climbing, rappelling, and snorkeling).

Unlike a typical Longdong climbing experience, “Cliff to Reef” allows you to take part in a variety of activities — including rappelling, traversing rock walls, and rock climbing. In the same day, you’ll also head to the ocean for snorkeling, completing a true “mountain-to-sea” full-day adventure.

Price

NT.3500

Activity Introdction

Duration

6 hours adventure

Difficulty

Intermediate

Longdong, located on Taiwan’s northeastern coast, is a rare place where the mountains meet the sea — allowing you to experience cliff rappelling, traversing, rock climbing in one day. — a true “high-altitude challenge” designed for adventurers.

Our “Cliff to Reef” experience begins with an exciting rappel from the top of Longdong’s cliffs down to the seaside rock platform — an entrance as cinematic as it gets. You’ll then challenge yourself on carefully chosen climbing routes, ranging from beginner-friendly to more technical lines, all while enjoying the stunning Pacific coastline as your backdrop.

Whether you’re here to challenge yourself, enjoy the scenery, or experience a one-of-a-kind outdoor adventure, this journey will become an unforgettable memory.

Trip Cancellation or Rescheduling Due to Weather

  • - Force Majeure: If the trip cannot proceed due to events such as a land typhoon warning, earthquake, cold wave, heavy rain advisory (as defined by the Central Weather Administration), road closures, or restrictions imposed by management authorities, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee.
  • - Poor Weather Forecast: If adverse weather such as continuous rain or an incoming cold front may affect the trip quality, we will conduct a comprehensive assessment. The final decision will be announced no later than 3 days before departure. If we decide to cancel the trip, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee.

Cancellation Due to Personal Reasons

  • - Cancel 14 days before departure: Refund minus a 5% administrative fee.
  • - Cancel 8–13 days before departure: 20% of the booking fee will be deducted.
  • - Cancel 3–7 days before departure: 30% of the booking fee will be deducted.
  • - Cancel within 2 days of departure: No refund.

Minimum Participant Requirement

  • - If the minimum participant number is not reached and the trip is canceled, participants may choose to reschedule for free or receive a refund with a 5% administrative fee deducted.
