Lead Climbing Course

This is a professional course that teaches you how to lead climb. You will learn how to belay correctly as a belayer, and how to stay safe as a climber — including placing quickdraws, clipping in, and finally lowering back to the ground. If you want to continue exploring more advanced climbing techniques, mastering this skill is essential.

Price

NT.4000

Activity Introdction

Duration

6 hours with 2 days

Difficulty

Intermediate. Need to familiar with toproping first

This is a professional course on lead climbing. All training and practice will take place directly at outdoor climbing sites, where you will learn how to handle various situations, such as lead falls and Z-clipping techniques. You must already be familiar with top-rope climbing skills before enrolling in this course.

Our goal is for you to become an independent climber who can confidently lead outdoors, recognize hazards, and protect both yourself and your climbing partner. This course also lays a solid foundation for those who wish to continue advancing in climbing in the future.

Trip Cancellation or Rescheduling Due to Weather

  • - Force Majeure: If the trip cannot proceed due to events such as a land typhoon warning, earthquake, cold wave, heavy rain advisory (as defined by the Central Weather Administration), road closures, or restrictions imposed by management authorities, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee.
  • - Poor Weather Forecast: If adverse weather such as continuous rain or an incoming cold front may affect the trip quality, we will conduct a comprehensive assessment. The final decision will be announced no later than 3 days before departure. If we decide to cancel the trip, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee.

Cancellation Due to Personal Reasons

  • - Cancel 14 days before departure: Refund minus a 5% administrative fee.
  • - Cancel 8–13 days before departure: 20% of the booking fee will be deducted.
  • - Cancel 3–7 days before departure: 30% of the booking fee will be deducted.
  • - Cancel within 2 days of departure: No refund.

Minimum Participant Requirement
