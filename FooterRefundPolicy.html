Refund Policy 退費機制

1. Trip Cancellation or Rescheduling Due to Weather / 天候不佳取消或改期

In the event of poor weather, the trip may be canceled or rescheduled. / 若天候不佳，活動可能取消或更改日期。

2. Force Majeure / 不可抗因素

If the trip cannot proceed due to force majeure events such as a typhoon warning, earthquake, cold wave, heavy rain advisory (as defined by the Central Weather Bureau), road closures, or restrictions imposed by management authorities, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee from the total activity cost. / 若遇活動目的地陸上颱風警報、地震、寒流、豪雨特報(定義請參酌中央氣象署)、道路或管理單位封閉等不可抗因素，致使無法出團，扣除活動費用5%行政手續費後退費。

3. Poor Weather Forecast / 天氣預報不佳

If adverse weather conditions, such as continuous rain or an incoming cold front, may affect the trip quality, we will conduct a comprehensive assessment. The final decision on whether the trip will proceed will be announced no later than 3 days before departure, or the activity location may be adjusted to Big Cannon Cliff or Rehai. If we decide to cancel the trip, a refund will be issued after deducting a 5% administrative fee from the total activity cost. / 若天氣預報為陰雨綿綿、冷氣團來襲等可能影響出團品質之情況，我方將進行綜合評估，最遲於出發日前3日公告是否照常出團，或將活動地點調整至大砲岩或熱海岩場。如評估後取消出團，扣除活動費用5%行政手續費後退費。

4. Cancellation Due to Personal Reasons / 個人因素取消規定

Cancel 14 days before departure: Full refund, less a 5% administrative fee. / 出發前14天取消：扣除活動費用5%行政手續費後退費。

Cancel 8–13 days before departure: 20% of the booking fee will be deducted. / 出發前8–13天取消：扣除20%必要支出後退費。

Cancel 3–7 days before departure: 30% of the booking fee will be deducted. / 出發前3–7天取消：扣除30%必要支出後退費。

Cancel within 2 days of departure: No refund. / 出發前2天內取消：不退費。
