Rehai Climbing Adventure

Introdction Starting Point and Traffic Advices Pricing Food Safety Important Notes Cancellation Policy

Duration

5-6 hours rock climbing

Introdction

Rehai is located in Beitou, Taipei City, hidden in the forested slopes of Danfeng Mountain. It is one of the most popular outdoor climbing spots in northern Taiwan. The area offers many sport climbing routes, ranging mostly from 5.6 to 5.10b, with a few 5.11 routes, making it suitable for beginners and advanced climbers. From the top of the rock wall, you can enjoy stunning views of Beitou, Guandu Plain, and Guanyin Mountain. After climbing, relax in Beitou’s famous hot springs and enjoy delicious local food for a perfect outdoor adventure day.

Traffic

Our journey begins at Yinguang Lane, with a 15-minute walk from the trailhead to the climbing area. If you use public transportation, take the MRT to Xinbeitou Station and walk to the climbing area in about an hour.

Pricing

  • - The recommended group size is 2-3 people, with a fee of NT$9,000 per group.
  • - An additional NT$3,000 will be charged for each extra person, up to a maximum of 6 people.
  • - The price includes a professional guide who will lead the climb, set up routes, and introduce the natural environment and ecology of the area.

Food

Please bring your own food for the day. We will provide fresh local fruits and snacks (at no extra charge) for you to enjoy.

Safety

  • - Safety is our top priority.
  • - We will arrange insurance for each participant (Specific Activity Insurance: NT$2 million for accidental death and disability, NT$200,000 for injury medical expenses, no extra charge).
  • - We maintain a guide-to-participant ratio of 1:3.
  • - Each guide is an experienced professional climber, proficient in English, familiar with first aid, and equipped with emergency management skills.

Notes

  • - Please wear comfortable and breathable outdoor clothing. Don’t forget to bring sun protection (such as sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses) and about 1.5 liters of drinking water.
  • - We will provide harnesses, helmets, and climbing shoes, but if you have your own climbing gear, it’s recommended to bring your climbing shoes. Additionally, if you have any special requirements or considerations, please inform us in advance.

Cancellation

Once the activity is confirmed, we will immediately start preparations, so we hope to confirm as early as possible. If you choose to cancel after confirmation, the following cancellation fees will apply:

  • - Cancel 8 days before departure: Full refund
  • - Cancel 3-7 days before departure: 30% of the booking fee will be deducted as a handling fee
  • - Cancel 2 days before departure: No refund
  • - If the activity is postponed or canceled due to bad weather or other force majeure factors (e.g., road closures), we will provide a full refund.
