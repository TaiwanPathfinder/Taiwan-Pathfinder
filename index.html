Who we are

We are Taiwan Pathfinder—a team of outdoor professionals proudly born and raised in Taiwan. United by our passion for nature, we’re dedicated to sharing the beauty of Taiwan’s mountains and wilderness with the world.

We’ve seen how language barriers often prevent foreigners from truly exploring Taiwan’s outdoors. That’s why we’re here—to make adventures safe, accessible, and meaningful for everyone.

With certified skills and real-world experience, we guide, support, and inspire. Let us help you discover Taiwan, one trail at a time.
