Privacy Policy 隱私政策

1. Information Collection / 資訊收集

We collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, and emergency contact details for activity registration and communication. / 我們會收集報名及聯絡所需的個人資料，例如姓名、電子郵件、電話號碼與緊急聯絡人資訊。

2. Purpose of Data Use / 資料使用目的

Personal data is used solely for activity organization, safety management, insurance purposes, and event notifications. / 個人資料僅用於活動安排、安全管理、保險投保及活動通知。

3. Data Protection / 資料保護

We implement reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. / 我們採取合理措施保護個人資料，防止未經授權的存取、洩漏、變更或破壞。

4. Data Sharing / 資料分享

We do not share personal data with third parties without consent, except when required by law or for insurance processing. / 除法律規定或保險理賠需要外，我們不會在未經同意的情況下將個人資料提供給第三方。

5. Data Retention / 資料保存期限

Personal data will be retained only as long as necessary for activity administration and legal compliance. / 個人資料僅保存至活動結束並符合法律要求的期間。

6. Rights of Participants / 參加者權利

Participants may request to access, correct, delete, or withdraw consent for their personal data at any time. / 參加者可隨時查詢、更正、刪除或撤回其個人資料同意。

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and track site usage. / 我們的網站可能使用 Cookies 以提升使用者體驗及追蹤網站使用情況。

8. Changes to Privacy Policy / 隱私政策變更

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page. / 我們保留修改隱私政策的權利，更新將公布於本頁面。
