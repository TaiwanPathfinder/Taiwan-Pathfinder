Longdong Beginner Rock Climbing Experience

A fun and safe introduction to outdoor rock climbing at Longdong! Experience Taiwan’s stunning coastal cliffs while learning climbing basics from expert guides. Perfect for beginners who want a safe, exciting adventure in the outdoors.

Price

NT.3000

Activity Introduction

Duration

Half-day (3-4 hours)

Difficulty

Beginner Friendly

Join our Longdong Beginner Rock Climbing Experience for a fun and safe introduction to outdoor climbing. Learn the basics of climbing from expert guides while enjoying the breathtaking coastal cliffs of Taiwan.

Perfect for beginners and anyone looking for an exciting outdoor adventure, this experience combines safety, guidance, and stunning scenery for a memorable day out.

Our journey will start at either the Hemei Elementary School Parking Lot or the Xilingyan Temple Parking Lot. Public transportation is time-consuming, so we recommend driving yourself (free parking available) or arranging a private transfer from Taipei in advance (additional charges apply).

  • - Professional guide leading the climb and setting up routes
  • - Introduction to the natural environment and ecology of Longdong
  • - Climbing equipment: harness, helmet, and climbing shoes provided
  • - Travel insurance included
  • - Each participant is covered by Specific Activity Insurance: NT$2 million for accidental death and disability, NT$200,000 for injury medical expenses
  • - Guide-to-participant ratio: 2 guides for 10 participants
  • - Guides are experienced climbers, proficient in English, trained in first aid and emergency management
  • - All safety instructions must be followed at all times
  • - Wear comfortable, breathable outdoor clothing
  • - Bring sun protection (sunscreen, hat, sunglasses) and ~2 liters of drinking water
  • - Climbing shoes provided; if you have your own, bringing them is recommended
  • - Notify us in advance of any special requirements or health considerations

Please bring your own food for the day to enjoy during breaks while taking in the coastal views.

The recommended group size is 2–3 people per guide, with a fee of NT$9,000 per group. An additional NT$3,000 will be charged per extra participant, up to a maximum of 6 people. Tips for guides are welcome if you find their service exceptional.
