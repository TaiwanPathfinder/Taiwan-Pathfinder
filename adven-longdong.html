Longdong Climbing Adventure

Duration

5-6 hours rock climbing (excluding transportation time)

Difficulty

Beginner to Advanced

Introdction

Longdong, located on Taiwan's northeastern coast, is one of the country's most renowned rock climbing destinations. Known for its stunning coastal cliffs, Longdong offers a variety of routes suitable for climbers of all skill levels, ensuring that both beginners and advanced climbers can fully enjoy their experience here. The name "Longdong," meaning "Dragon's Cave," reflects the spectacular terrain, with towering cliffs directly facing the ocean, creating breathtaking views.

This climbing experience not only challenges your heights but also allows you to enjoy the beautiful views of the Pacific Ocean. The sea breeze and the sound of waves crashing against the rocks make this climbing adventure truly unforgettable. The diverse range of routes lets climbers push their limits while soaking in the magnificent surroundings.

In addition to climbing, the area boasts rich natural beauty. Nearby coastal trails are perfect for leisurely walks, and the clear waters are ideal for snorkeling. Whether you are an experienced climber or looking to try outdoor climbing for the first time, Longdong provides a comprehensive outdoor experience.

Starting Point and Traffic Advices

Our journey will start at either the Hemei(和美) Elementary School Parking Lot or the 西靈巖 Temple Parking Lot.

Due to the longer travel time by public transportation, we recommend driving yourself (free parking available) or arranging a private transfer from Taipei in advance (additional charges apply).

Pricing

- The recommended group size is 2-3 people, with a fee of NT$9,000 per group.

- An additional NT$3,000 will be charged for each extra person, up to a maximum of 6 people.

- The price includes a professional guide who will lead the climb, set up routes, and introduce the natural environment and ecology of the area.

- If you find the guide’s service exceptional, tips are welcome as a gesture of appreciation.

Food

Please bring your own food for the day.
We will provide fresh local fruits and snacks (at no extra charge) for you to enjoy while taking in the stunning coastal views.

Safety

  • - Safety is our top priority.
  • - We will arrange insurance for each participant (Specific Activity Insurance: NT$2 million for accidental death and disability, NT$200,000 for injury medical expenses, no extra charge).
  • - We maintain a guide-to-participant ratio of 1:3.
  • - Each guide is an experienced professional climber, proficient in English, familiar with first aid, and equipped with emergency management skills.

Important Notes

  • - Please wear comfortable and breathable outdoor clothing.
  • - Don’t forget to bring sun protection (such as sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses) and about 2 liters of drinking water.
  • - We will provide harnesses, helmets, and climbing shoes, but if you have your own climbing gear, it’s recommended to bring your climbing shoes.
  • - Additionally, if you have any special requirements or considerations, please inform us in advance.

Cancellation Policy

Once the activity is confirmed, we will immediately start preparations, so we hope to confirm as early as possible.
If you choose to cancel after confirmation, the following cancellation fees will apply:

  • - Cancel 8 days before departure: Full refund
  • - Cancel 3-7 days before departure: 30% of the booking fee will be deducted as a handling fee
  • - Cancel 2 days before departure: No refund
  • - If the activity is postponed or canceled due to bad weather or other force majeure factors (e.g., road closures), we will provide a full refund.
