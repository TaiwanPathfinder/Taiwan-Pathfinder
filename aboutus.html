About Taiwan Pathfinder

Founded in 2024 — 100% Local, Taiwanese-Owned

Taiwan Pathfinder was founded in 2024 by a team of passionate outdoor professionals, all proudly born and raised in Taiwan. Unlike many tour operators, we are truly local guides who grew up in these mountains, with a deep connection to the land, culture, and people. Our mission is to share the breathtaking beauty of Taiwan’s wilderness with adventurers from around the world.

English-Friendly Hiking, Trekking & Climbing Tours

We understand that language barriers often make it difficult for foreigners to explore Taiwan’s stunning mountains. That’s why all our hiking, trekking, and rock climbing tours are fully guided in English—clear, approachable, and inclusive. We provide not just guidance, but also cultural and language bridges, making outdoor adventures in Taiwan safe, accessible, and meaningful.

Certified Mountain Guides & Outdoor Experts

Our team holds internationally recognized certifications in mountain guiding, rock climbing, skiing, and wilderness first aid. With years of hands-on experience, we combine technical expertise with a heartfelt commitment to safety, education, and environmental stewardship. Whether you join us for a short day hike or a multi-day expedition, you’ll be guided by trusted professionals.

More Than Just Adventure — A Cultural Journey

At Taiwan Pathfinder, we believe that mountains are not just places to conquer, but spaces to learn, grow, and connect. Along the trail, you’ll also experience Taiwan’s unique culture, history, and traditions, making each adventure both a physical and cultural journey.

Join Us to Discover Taiwan’s Great Outdoors

Whether you’re a beginner hiker, a seasoned trekker, or a climber seeking new challenges, Taiwan Pathfinder is here to guide you. Our goal is to inspire personal growth, celebrate nature, and make Taiwan’s mountains open to all. Let us be your trusted companion on your next adventure in Taiwan.
