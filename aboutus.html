About Taiwan Pathfinder

We are Taiwan Pathfinder —a team of passionate outdoor professionals, all proudly born and raised in Taiwan. Brought together by a deep love for nature and adventure, we strive to share the breathtaking beauty of Taiwan’s wilderness with the world.

Over the years, we’ve noticed that many foreigners are eager to explore Taiwan’s incredible mountains and outdoor experiences, yet often face language barriers and a lack of accessible resources. That’s why we’re committed not only to guiding, but also to bridging the cultural and linguistic gaps that prevent people from truly experiencing Taiwan’s great outdoors.

With diverse expertise and internationally recognized certifications in mountain guiding, rock climbing, skiing, and wilderness first aid, our team combines technical skill with a heartfelt commitment to safety and education. We believe that the mountains are not just places to conquer, but spaces to learn, grow, and connect—with nature and with ourselves.

At Taiwan Pathfinder, our mission is to guide people safely into the outdoors, inspire personal challenge, and celebrate the natural beauty of Taiwan. Whether you’re new to the wilderness or a seasoned adventurer, we’re here to be your trusted companions on the journey.
