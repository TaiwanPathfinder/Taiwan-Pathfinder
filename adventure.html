Longdong, located on Taiwan's northeastern coast, is one of the country's most renowned rock climbing destinations. Known for its stunning coastal cliffs, Longdong offers a variety of routes suitable for climbers of all skill levels, ensuring that both beginners and advanced climbers can fully enjoy their experience here.
